Partners in Pen

Host will hide any random object and describe the hidden object and participants will draw it according to the description.The closest person wins the game.

Home Beg Borrow

We can make a list of items or ask participants for video or pic which they can find at home and one who brings fastest wins.

Memory Game

Some objects are shown on screen for 5 seconds to both teams, each person from each team are given chance to tell one object they saw, if wrong team loses points.

Outburst

There would be two teams playing against each other and they would be provided with a particular topic . They are required to make a list based on the given topic and yell that out. The team who yell that list first would win the match.

Word Crossword

Crossword like in newspaper but the hints can be creative maybe a puzzle or riddle.

Dumb Charades

It will be a two round game. In the first round one of the participants will lip sync a famous dialogue and the rest guess. In the second round they would have to enact and guess the movie.

Irrelevant Answer

Two Persons can play this game and they have to give irrelevant answer to the question asked by us and another one has to guess it. For example what is your favourite colour ? Ans can be anything but not a colour.

7 in 7

A random word will appear on the screen and each participant will have 7 seconds to name 7 words connected to the given word.

Mystery Rooms

We can plan mystery rooms on google forms .You can only move forward on the next page when you have solved all the previous clues. It can also be a team participation and can also be based on a story. I have already played one based on Harry Potter.

Scavenger Hunt

We can make some PPTs and keep a password to each PPT except the first one, and each PPT will contain the clue to open the next PPT and on the last PPT we can add some gift voucher or some goodie.

Online beg borrow

Participants will be given riddles and answers to those riddles will be some items that can be found at home

Tambola

By zoom we can conduct this by one person saying numbers n sending pics of tickets to everyone which they can draw or get a printout
